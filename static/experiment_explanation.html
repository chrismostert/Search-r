Experiment explanation

In a week an important debate is taking place. You are part of the debate preparation research team. Your task is to find as many documents as possible which are relevant to the given topic. The topic you are researching will be introduced by a short descriptive text, and you will be given a definition on what defines a 'relevant document' for the given topic. Try to find as many relevant documents as possible. Aim for at least a number of documents where you feel that it is enough to be well prepared for a debate.
